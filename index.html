Get In Touch

If you like our work and you would like to get in touch us just send us a short message and click the SEND button. We will answer your inquiry in the shortest time possible.

Very often patients ask us to tell them all about the treatment options and pricing during this initial contact, which is not always possible. That is the reason why we have created this web site so you can see all the options, including the time spent and price range for a case presented.

We strongly encourage you to come and visit us, so we can provide you with the professional service at our clinic. Feel free to call us at (+385) 1 3893 376 for all the information.
